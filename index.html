    Nissi Roto 5002 Rotogravure Printing Machine

    The Nissi Roto 5002 is a multi-color rotogravure printing machine manufactured by Alpha Roto Machines Pvt. Ltd. It is designed for printing on flexible materials such as BOPP, PET, CPP, Nylon, LDPE, and other laminates. This machine uses Mechanical Line Shaft (MLS) technology and is suitable for high-quality, short to medium-run printing jobs.

    NISSI SL 3010 Solventless Lamination Machine

    The product shown in the image is the NISSI SL 3010 solventless lamination machine. It is manufactured by Alpha Roto Machines Pvt. Ltd. This machine is designed for solventless lamination on flexible packaging materials. It is suitable for short and medium-run applications.
