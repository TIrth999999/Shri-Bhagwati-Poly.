About Bhagwati Poly Pack

Providing you the highest quality

Established in 1991, Shri Bhagwati Poly Pack is a leading Indian manufacturer of multilayer films and flexible laminates. Powered by advanced Rotogravure printing, we deliver custom packaging solutions designed to protect, preserve, and promote your products.

Our Products & Services:

  • Flexible Laminates
  • Rigid Films
  • Multilayer Packaging Complexes
  • Rotogravure Printed Rolls & Pouches
  • Stand-up, zipper, gusseted, D-cut, and custom pouches
