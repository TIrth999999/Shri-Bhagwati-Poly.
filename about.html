Director

Under the visionary leadership of our Director, Shri Bhagwati Polypack has emerged as a leading manufacturer of flexible packaging solutions in India. With over 30 years of industry expertise, our Director has been instrumental in driving innovation, maintaining quality standards, and expanding our market reach across the nation.

Our Director's commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted partner for businesses seeking premium packaging solutions. Through strategic investments in cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices, we continue to set new benchmarks in the packaging industry.

Chairman

Our Chairman, Shri Ghanshyambhai Desai, is the founding force behind Shri Bhagwati Polypack. With his entrepreneurial spirit and deep industry knowledge, he established the company in 1991 with a vision to revolutionize the flexible packaging landscape in India.

Under his guidance, we have grown from a small manufacturing unit to a renowned name in packaging solutions, serving diverse industries with ISO 9001:2015 and FSSAI certifications. His leadership continues to inspire our team to deliver innovative, sustainable, and high-quality packaging solutions.
